Enrique Sanz-Gadea

Architect trained at ETSAM with professional experience at Lacaton & Vassal in Paris, contributing to large-scale cultural, residential and urban projects from concept design to competition submission. Skilled in architectural design, 3D modelling, spatial analysis and visual communication, with a strong interest in environmental strategy, technology and complex systems.

This archive is a living platform for sharing academic, professional and personal work. It is meant to make projects available, but also to trace affinities between them: recurring questions, principles and threads that connect different works over time. In that sense, it is both a way of presenting myself and a tool for understanding my own practice.
